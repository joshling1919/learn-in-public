## [[details-element]] 

[Create a collapsible block in an HTML doc][collapsible] by using the
`<details>` and `<summary>` elements:

```html

<details>
  <summary>Expandable title!</summary>
  <!-- space required here -->

  content inside of the collapsible block
</details>

```
